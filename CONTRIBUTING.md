# Contributing

Thank you for helping keep this list current.

## What to add

A good addition is a URL that:
- Is publicly accessible without a paywall or mandatory account
- Contains real candidate-reported interview questions or structured prep material for software engineering roles
- Has not already been added

## What not to add

- Paywalled resources unless they have a meaningful free tier (note the limitation in your PR)
- Sites that aggregate questions without attribution (we link to primary sources)
- Referral links or affiliate links
- Your own project or company unless it is genuinely well-known in the prep community

## Process

1. Fork this repo
2. Add your entry to the correct category in README.md
3. Follow the existing format: `- **[Name](URL)** -- One-sentence description.`
4. Open a pull request with a brief explanation of what you are adding and why it belongs

## The full database

This list covers publicly available sources. For a live, searchable database of 59,970+ real reported interview questions across 2,000+ companies, see [LeakCode](https://leakcode.dev). LeakCode includes content translated from Chinese-language forums (1Point3Acres, Nowcoder) that no English-language tool covers.
